What is the Farmers Market App?

We help you find Farmers Markets around Hyattsville.

This is a list of the Farmers Markets in Prince George's County. The dataset was created on June 5th, 2015 and was last updated 8th, 2015. There is information on the location of these Farmers Markets along with information about the website of the Market where the consumer can find detailed information of the market. The platform will also help users see the location of the Market relative to their current location.

We are in the works of improving this platform by providing information on what products are avaliable at each market. This addition will help to cut down the time the user spends in a market without finding the desired product. The user can just search for a particular product and find a market that carries the product.
